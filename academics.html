Academics

Explore our diverse programs and educational offerings designed to nurture, challenge, and inspire students at every level.

Programs Offered

Our programs are tailored to ensure holistic growth and development for all students:

  • Pre-School: Focused on basic literacy, social skills, and foundational education for young learners.
  • Elementary: Core subjects, skill development, and extracurricular activities to foster academic and personal growth.
  • Secondary: Advanced programs, electives, and specialized tracks to prepare students for higher education and careers.

Pre-School

Nurturing young minds with a curriculum that encourages curiosity, creativity, and early learning milestones.

  • Nursery
  • Kindergarten
  • Preparatory

Elementary Education

Comprehensive education for Grades 1 to 6 that focuses on core academic subjects, critical thinking, and extracurricular activities.

  • Core Subjects: Mathematics, Science, English, Filipino, and Social Studies
  • Grading System: Assessment based on performance, participation, and periodic exams
  • Extracurricular Activities: Art, Music, Physical Education, and Clubs

Junior High School

Advanced learning for Grades 7 to 10, preparing students with foundational knowledge and skills for specialized senior high tracks.

  • Subjects: Core curriculum with options for enrichment and electives
  • Activities: Science fairs, student government, and club events

Senior High School

Specialized tracks for Grades 11 and 12, designed to equip students with knowledge and skills for college or career readiness.

  • STEM: Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics
  • ABM: Accountancy, Business, and Management
  • HUMSS: Humanities and Social Sciences
  • TVL: Technical-Vocational-Livelihood track
