Welcome to Monlimar Development Academy Inc.

"Fulfilling lives, Molding character, Building future."

Introduction

Monlimar Development Academy, Inc. is an institution of excellence, character, and service that fulfills dreams, molds character, and builds the future of all learners and staff involved in the education process.

At Monlimar Development Academy, we believe in creating a dynamic learning environment where students feel valued, supported, and motivated to explore their passions. Our dedicated staff and innovative curriculum ensure that every child receives a quality education tailored to their unique needs and aspirations.

Our Mission

To ensure that all our students develop knowledge, skills, proper behavior, and attitudes to become successful individuals and responsible citizens. We aim to promote excellence in a caring environment and prepare each student in partnership with stakeholders and the community.

Our Vision

Monlimar Development Academy Inc. is committed to improving learning and producing students with high academic achievement, balanced and well-molded character, and a willingness to serve their country and fellowmen.

Our Goal

  • Respect self, others, and the environment.
  • Participate productively and responsibly in a rapidly changing society.
  • Communicate effectively.
  • Use cooperative and independent learning styles to accomplish goals.
  • Apply problem-solving techniques in real-world situations.
